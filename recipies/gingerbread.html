Gingerbread

Every family needs a plate of gingerbread cookies around the holidays!

While some families find it easier to get the pre-packaged stuff at the supermarket, making Gingerbread men with the family makes the experience a whole lot sweeter. To start you are going to need the following ingredients

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 2 teaspoons of ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 cup of softened butter
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of molasses
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • and any frosting/toppings for decorating
  1. Mix the flour, cinnamon, baking soda, nutmeg, and salt in one bowl
  2. In another bowl, beat the butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy
  3. Add molasses, egg, and vanilla and mix well
  4. Gradually mix in the first bowl untill its all well mixed
  5. press dough into a flat disk and wrap it in plastic to refrigerate overnight
  6. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  7. roll dough out on a floured surface
  8. cut out cookies and place 1 inch apart on ungreased baking sheets
  9. bake for around 10 minutes
  10. let the cookies cool and decorate
