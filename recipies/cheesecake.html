Cheesecake

A perfect centerpiece for your christmas desert table

While many people may think that cheesecake is a bit hard to make, and those voices may be right, a plate full of cheesecake is just to good to pass up for any reason. The recipe can be split into 2 parts, those being the crust, and the filling itself. You are going to need the following.

  • Around 10 Graham Crackers
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 5 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 2 pounds of cream cheese
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons of lemon juice
  • and 3 eggs
  1. Grind the crackers into a dust with a food processor or blender
  2. Mix together with the melted butter and granulated sugar
  3. Pack mixture into the pie tin you're using
  4. Pre-bake the crust in an oven for around 18 minutes, slowly going from 300 degrees F to 350 degrees F
  5. get a bowl and place the cream cheese in for mixing
  6. Mix in the cup of sugar into our cheese, followed by the sour cream, vanilla extract, lemon juice, and end by beating in the eggs one at a time
  7. Add our filling into the graham cracker tin
  8. Place the cake into a water bath, by putting it in a roasting pan full of hot water, and baking both to steam the filling and prevent cracks
  9. Open your oven door for a while to let the cake cool down naturally, then cool on counter, and then move to fridge overnight
